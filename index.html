Hello !!

Welcome to my personal page. I am a computational physicists with broad range of experience in programming languages, high-performance computing, simulation, mathematical-modeling and data-driven solutions to understand material properties for various industrial applications.

At present, I work at ComScore Inc. as a senior data scientist. In my current role, I work with advertisement data to understand cross-device overlap, cross-media overlap and user privacy-centric advertisement measurement across devices and online platforms.

I am passionate about learning new programming languages, statistical methods, computational tools and automating data-processing.

In my free-time, I love to read books, go hiking, play cricket, play soccer and hangout with my wife and friends.
